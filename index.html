    FAQs.

    Q: Do you require a walk thru with my pet before I book service?


    A: We'd very much like to meet you and your canine before you book service to confirm it'd be a great fit for all parties.


    Q: Where are you located?


    A: We're located off Highway 212 in Cologne, MN. Near City Hall.


    Q: What is your boarding enviornment like?


    A: We're a small start-up operating in a single family home. Our outdoor area is a fenced in half acre. Our interior space provides ample square footage.


    Q: Do you provide food, chew toys or kennels?


    A: We can provide Iams Active Health (large and small chunks) for an additional cost. We can provide chew toys and kennels for no addtional cost.
